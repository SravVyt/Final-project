Double Tomato Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 6 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, stems removed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 french baguette
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions
  1. Preheat your oven by setting it on broil.
  2. Mix together the plum tomatos, sundried tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Set aside for 10 minutes
  3. Next, cut the french baguette into slices 3/4 of an inch.
  4. Spread the baguette slices in a single layer on a baking sheet, and broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until slightly brown.
  5. Evenly divide the tomato mixture(from step 2) on top of the baguette slices. Top the baguette slices with mozzerella cheese.
  6. Finally, broil once again for 5 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  7. ENJOY!

Nachos and Guacamole dip

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados(peel, remove pit and mash)
  • 1 lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • optional: 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • Nachos

Directions
  1. Mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt in a bowl. The slowly mix in the onion, cilantro, tomatoes, garlic. Add in the cayenne pepper (Optional).
  2. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 1 hour to get the best flavor.
  3. Have your nachos ready, dig in and ENJOY!

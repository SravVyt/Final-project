Italian sausage with farfalle and broccoli rabe

Ingredients

  • one 16 ounce package of farfalle pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/8 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 bunch of trimmed broccoli rabe
  • 1 1/2 cups of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of butter>

Directions
  1. Lightly salt a large pot of water and bring it to a boil over high heat. Add the farfalle, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until firm to the bite. Then drain.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat heat the olive oil, then add in the Italian sausage until it has a crumbly texture and is no longer pink(about 5 minutes).
  3. Then stir in the garlic and continue cooking the mixture until the sausage starts browning(about 5 minutes),
  4. Pour out the excess grease. and then pour in the red pepper flakes and the chicken broth. Once again bring the pan to a boil over high heat, and then add in the broccoli rabe and cover the skillet. Cook the broccoli rabe for 4 minutes or until it is tender.
  5. When the broccoli rabe is done cooking slowly stir in the butter, parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Do this until the meat sauce has thickened.
  6. Mix the broccoli rabe and Italian sausage mixture with the farfalle.
  7. ENJOY!

Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 lb. of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1 cups of frozen peas, defrosted
  • 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 large egg, whisked
  • sriracha, for serving
  • 2 tablespoon sliced green onions

Directions
  1. Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, add garlic and stir for one minute. Then add carrots and peppers and sauté (fry quickly using little oil or fat) for 3 minutes.
  3. Then add the shrimp and cook for about 4 minutes, stir occasionally.
  4. Next stir in the rice and peas and add soy sauce and sesame oil as a seasoning, sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Push rice to one half of the skillet and add the egg. Stir egg constantly until almost fully cooked, then mix into rice mixture.
  6. Decorate with sriracha and green onions and serve.
  7. ENJOY!

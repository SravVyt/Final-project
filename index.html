Double Tomato Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 6 plum tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, stems removed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 french baguette
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions
  1. Preheat your oven by setting it on broil.
  2. Mix together th plum tomators, sundried tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Set aside for 10 minutes
  3. next cut the french baguette into slices 3/4 of an inch.
  4. Spread the baguette slices in a single layeron a baking sheet, and broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until slightly brown.
  5. Evenly divide the tomato mixture(from step 2) on top of the baguette slices. Top the baugette slices with mozzerella cheese.
  6. Finally broil once again for 5 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  7. ENJOY!

Nacho and Guacamole dip

Ingredients

  • 3 avacados(peel, remove pit and mash)
  • 1 lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • optional: 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper
  • Nachos

Directions
  1. Mash together the avacados, lime juice, and salt in a bowl. The slowly mix in th onion, cilantro, tomatoes, garlic. Add in the cayenne pepper (Optional).
  2. Serve immediatley or refrigerate for 1 hour to get the best flavor.
  3. Have your nachos ready, dig in and ENJOY!

Italian sausage with farfalle and broccoli rabe

Ingredients

  • one 16 ounce package of farfalle pasta
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 1/2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/8 teaspoon of crushed red peper flakes
  • 1 bunch of trimmed broccoli rabe
  • 1 1/2 cups of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of butter>

Directions
  1. Lightly salt a large pot of water and bring it to a boil over high heat. Add the farfalle, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until firm to the bite. Then drain.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat heat the olive oil, then add in the the Italian sausage until it has a crumbly texture and is no longer pink(about 5 minutes).
  3. Then stir in the garlic ad continue cooking mixture until the sausage starts browning(about 5 minutes),
  4. Pour out the excess grease. and then pour in the red pepperflakes and the chicken broth. Once again bring the pan to a boil over high heat,and then add in the broccoli rabe and cover the skillet. Cook the broccoli rabe for 4 minutes or until it is tender.
  5. When the broccoli rabe os done cooking slowly stir in the butter, paremesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Do this until the meat sauce has thickened.
  6. Mix the broccolli rabe and Italian sausage mixture with the farfalle.
  7. ENJOY!

Shrimp Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 lb. of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups of cooked white rice
  • 1 cups of frozen peas, defrosted
  • 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 large egg, whisked
  • sriracha, for serving
  • 2 tablespoon sliced green onions

Directions
  1. Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, add garlic and stir for one minute. Then add carrots and peppers and sauté (fry quickly using little oil or fat) for 3 minutes.
  3. Then add the shrimp and cook for about 4 minutes, stir occasionally.
  4. Next stir in the rice and peas and add soy sauce and sesame oil as seasoning, sauté for 2 minutes.
  5. Push rice to one half of the skillet and add the egg. Stir egg constantly until almost fully cooked, then mix into rice mixture.
  6. Decorate with sriracha and green onions and serve.
  7. ENJOY!

Macarons

Ingredients

  • almond flour
  • confectioners' sugar
  • room temperature egg whites

Directions
  1. First, in a blender you mix the alomnd flour and confectioners’ sugar, until it has a fine texture.
  2. Next you beat the egg whites until a stiff peak forms(Let the egg whites sit overnight)
  3. Then lightly beat the sifted sugar into the egg whites. Then put the almond flour/confectioners’ sugar blend into the egg white mixture.(The batter should be thick, off-white, glossy, and sticky.)
  4. Pipe the batter onto baking sheets in small round circles.Before baking let the piped circles sit for a bit.
  5. The put them in oven for 10 minutes at 300-350 degrees.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes, and then add filling.
  7. ENJOY!

Chocolate Souffle

Ingredients

  • Room temperature unsalted butter(for Ramekin dish)
  • 1/4 cup sugar keep some extra aside for the Ramekin
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 onces of somewhat sweet choclate ( finely chopped) or 1 cup of chocolate chips
  • 3 largeclightly beaten eggyolks
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cream of tartar

Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, lightly butter Ramekin, and coat with sugar. Put dish on a baking sheet.
  2. In a large heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, combine chocolate, vanilla, and 1/4 cup water. Stir for ten minutes or until choclate is completley melted and smooth. Cool for 20 minutes or until room temperature.
  3. Stir egg yolks into chocolate mixture thouroughly, set aside souffle base.
  4. Then use an electric mixer to beat the egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form or for 2 minutes. Then slowly add sugar bit by bit until stiff and shiny peaks form or for about 5 minutes, make sure not to overbeat.
  5. Fold egg white mixture into souffle base with a rubber spatula, gently lift up base from bottom of the bowl.
  6. Transfer the mixture in to the Ramekin and smooth top, while doing this make sure not to get batter on top edge of Ramekin. Bake until the souffle is puffed up (30 to 35 minutes) Remove from oven. Make sure not to open oven during the first 25 minutes.
  7. Optional: Dust the souffle with confectioners' sugar.
  8. Serve immediatley, otherwise the souffle will lose its height.
  9. ENJOY!

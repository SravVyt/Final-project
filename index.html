Macarons

Ingredients

  • almond flour
  • confectioners’sugar
  • room temperature egg whites

Directions
  • First, in a blender you mix the alomnd flour and confectioners’sugar, until it has a fine texture.
  • Next you beat the egg whites until a stiff peak forms(Let the egg whites sit overnight)
  • Then lightly beat the sifted sugar into the egg whites. Then put the almond flour/confectioners’ sugar blend into the egg white mixture.(The batter should be thick, off-white, glossy, and sticky.)
  • Pipe the batter onto baking sheets in small round circles.Before baking let the piped circles sit for a bit.
  • The put them in oven for 10 minutes at 300-350 degrees.
  • Let it cool for a few minutes, and then add filling.
  • ENJOY!

    Chocolate Souffle

    Ingredients

    • Room temperature unsalted butter(for Ramekin dish)
    • 1/4 cup sugar keep some extra aside for the Ramekin
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 8 onces of somewhat sweet choclate ( finely chopped) or 1 cup of chocolate chips
    • 3 largeclightly beaten eggyolks
    • 4 large egg whites
    • 1/4 teaspoon of cream of tartar

    Directions
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, lightly butter Ramekin, and coat with sugar. Put dish on a baking sheet
    2. In a large heatproof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, combine chocolate, vanilla, and 1/4 cup water. Stir for ten minutes or until choclate is completley melted and smooth. Cool for 20 minutes or until room temperature.
    3. Stir egg yolks into chocolate mixture thouroughly, set aside souffle base.
    4. Then use an electric mixer to beat the egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form or for 2 minutes. Then slowly add sugar bit by bit until stiff and shiny peaks form or for about 5 minutes, make sure not to overbeat.
    5. Fold egg white mixture into souffle base with a rubber spatula, gently lift up base from bottom of the bowl.
    6. Transfer the mixture in to the Ramekin and smooth top, while doing this make sure not to get batter on top edge of Ramekin. Bake until the souffle is puffed up (30 to 35 minutes) Remove from oven. Make sure not to open oven during the first 25 minutes.
    7. Optional: Dust the souffle with confectioners' sugar.
    8. Serve immediatley, otherwise the souffle will lose its height.
    9. ENJOY!

    Double Tomato Bruschetta

    Ingredients

    • 6 plum tomatoes, chopped
    • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil
    • 3 cloves minced garlic
    • 3 cloves minced garlic
    • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
    • 1/4 cup fresh basil, stems removed
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 french baguette
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

    Directions
    1. Preheat your oven by setting it on broil.
    2. Mix together th plum tomators, sundried tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, basil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Set aside for 10 minutes
    3. next cut the french baguette into slices 3/4 of an inch.
    4. Spread the baguette slices in a single layeron a baking sheet, and broil for 1 to 2 minutes or until slightly brown.
    5. Evenly divide the tomato mixture(from step 2) on top of the baguette slices. Top the baugette slices with mozzerella cheese.
    6. Finally broil once again for 5 minutes or until cheese has melted.
    7. ENJOY!
